Does peace affect happiness of people and to which extent?

As we look at the level of happiness around the world, we could not help but be concerned about the peace of these countries. This is why there is the "Global Peace Index", as an index that assesses the level of peace and safety in different countries of the world. The index is assessed based on various metrics and data, such as violent attacks, conflicts, inequality, corruption, police action and other security factors. Based on these data, each country is assigned a place in the global list of Global Peace Index. The period between 2015 and 2021 that we have chosen to visualize is not random, as major social, political and economic events have shaken the planet and affected the "Global Peace Index". Some of them are civil wars, loss of lives and refugees, acts of terrorism, political conflicts, terrorist attacks and coups that change the global peace and safety map.
